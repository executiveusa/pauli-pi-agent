# Sales Call Script

## Purpose

Help PI prepare the user for a sales conversation.

## Call Flow

1. Confirm the business model.
2. Confirm the current lead/customer workflow.
3. Ask where leads are lost.
4. Ask what follow-up is manual.
5. Ask what software feels too complicated.
6. Show the demo.
7. Tie every screen to money, time, trust, or simplicity.
8. Explain the monthly value loop.
9. Offer setup + subscription.
10. Close with a low-friction next step.

## General Discovery Questions

- What happens after someone fills out your form?
- How fast do you usually follow up?
- Where do the leads go now?
- Do you know which leads are serious?
- Do you use WhatsApp, email, phone, or all three?
- What do customers ask repeatedly?
- What part of the workflow takes too much time?
- What type of customer is most valuable?
- What would make this easier every week?
- What follow-up do you wish happened automatically?

## Real Estate / Foreign Buyer Questions

- What does a serious foreign buyer usually ask first?
- How do you qualify budget, timeline, and location?
- How do you build trust before the first call?
- Do you lose leads because buyers are confused about the process?
- Do you have verified partners for legal, notary, construction, insurance, and property management?

## Eco-Tour / Hotel Questions

- Do guests usually book one thing or multiple experiences?
- Do you currently package rooms, tours, transport, meals, and activities together?
- How do you follow up with travelers before arrival?
- What premium packages sell best?
- Do hotels send you referrals, and can you track them?

## Plumber / HVAC Questions

- How many leads come in after hours?
- How do customers send photos or service details?
- Do you offer recurring maintenance?
- How do you remind customers about seasonal service?
- What happens when several urgent requests come in at once?

## Anime / Digital Products Questions

- What digital assets already exist?
- Which products could be sold repeatedly?
- Do you have a lead magnet?
- How do buyers receive files?
- Is there a drop calendar or campaign flow?

## Medical / Dental Tourism Questions

- What information do patients need before they feel comfortable traveling?
- How do you qualify treatment interest?
- What documents or photos do coordinators need?
- What follow-up happens after the first inquiry?
- Do you have English/Spanish intake?

## Destination Wedding / Event Questions

- What makes a serious event inquiry?
- How do you qualify date, budget, guest count, and venue needs?
- How do you recommend vendors?
- Do you track referral partners?
- What follow-up happens after the first inquiry?

## Demo Walkthrough Script

For each screen, say:

> "This screen is where [customer action happens]. For you, that means [business outcome]."

End the walkthrough with:

> "The goal is not a one-time build. The goal is a system that keeps working for you — capturing leads, following up automatically, reminding customers, tracking referrals, and improving every month. That is worth more than a website you pay for once and forget."

## Objection Handling

**"We already have a website."**
> "This is not about replacing your website. It is about adding the system behind it that captures leads, follows up automatically, and tracks what is working."

**"We are not ready right now."**
> "Totally understand. Can I send you the demo link so you have it when the timing is right?"

**"That seems expensive."**
> "One [lead type] that converts through this system covers the monthly cost. After that it is pure margin."
