# Sales Call Script: {{business_name}}

## Opening (2 min)

"I wanted to show you this because I think the opportunity is not just design. It is the system behind how leads come in, get qualified, get followed up with, and turn into revenue."

## Discovery (5 min)

1. "What happens after someone fills out your form or contacts you?"
2. "How fast do you usually follow up?"
3. "Where do the leads go right now?"
4. "Do you know which leads are actually serious?"
5. "What follow-up is manual that you wish was automatic?"

## Demo Walkthrough (8 min)

**Screen 1: Customer-facing intake**
> "This is where a customer comes in. Instead of them just sending an email or calling, they fill this out. For you, that means [specific outcome]."

**Screen 2: Owner dashboard**
> "This is your dashboard. You can see new leads, their status, and what needs follow-up today. That saves you [X hours] per week."

**Screen 3: Automations**
> "Everything after this is automatic. Follow-ups go out on day 1, day 3, day 7. No manual emails. That builds better [trust/conversion/retention]."

**Screen 4: Reporting**
> "At the end of the month, you see how many leads came in, how many converted, where they came from. That tells you what is working."

## Close (3 min)

"The goal is not a one-time build. The goal is a system that keeps working for you — capturing leads, following up automatically, reminding customers, tracking referrals, and improving every month. That is worth more than a website you pay for once and forget."

## Pricing Close

"Setup is $[X]. Then it is $[Y]/month for everything: hosting, maintenance, the dashboard, automations, reporting, and monthly improvements. One [lead type] that converts covers the monthly cost. After that it is pure margin."

## Objection Handling

**"We already have a website."**
> "This is not about replacing your website. It is about adding the system behind it that captures leads, follows up automatically, and tracks what is working."

**"We are not ready right now."**
> "Totally understand. Can I send you the demo link so you have it when the timing is right?"

**"That seems expensive."**
> "One [lead type] that converts through this system covers the monthly cost. After that it is pure margin."

**"Can we just do a one-time build?"**
> "Sure, but the value is in the system running every day. A one-time build gets stale. The subscription includes monthly improvements and keeps the system working for you."
