# Revenue Systems Workflow

## Input

**Minimum:**
- Business name or website URL

**Optional:**
- Industry
- Location
- Google Business profile
- Social links
- Known pain point
- Existing client relationship
- Desired price point
- Target niche
- Whether this is a Pauli Sent Me OS partner

## Process

### 1. Business Snapshot

Collect:

- Business type
- Offer
- Target customer
- Website quality
- Booking/contact path
- Social proof
- Review strength
- Current customer journey
- Trust signals
- Lead capture quality
- Follow-up quality
- Repeat purchase potential

### 2. Funnel Map

Map the customer path:

```text
Visitor → Trust Check → Inquiry/Booking → Appointment/Sale → Follow-up → Repeat Sale/Referral
```

For Pauli Sent Me OS partners:

```text
Directory Page → QR Scan → Partner Offer → "Pauli sent me" → Lead Captured → Partner Follow-up → Referral Logged
```

### 3. Pain Detection

Look for:

- Missed repeat bookings
- Manual follow-up
- Weak intake forms
- No CRM
- No reminder system
- No quote builder
- No compliance tracking
- No personalized customer experience
- No post-sale workflow
- No referral tracking
- No package builder
- No foreign-buyer trust layer
- No multilingual intake
- No WhatsApp-first follow-up
- No proof/review capture
- No partner attribution

### 4. Pain Scoring

Score from 1–5:

- Revenue impact
- Frequency
- Owner stress
- Ease of demo
- Repeatability
- Trust impact
- Puerto Vallarta/local advantage
- Pauli Sent Me OS fit

### 5. System Selection

Choose the system with the highest total score.

The system must be explainable in one sentence.

**Example:**

> We help Puerto Vallarta eco-tour operators sell more premium packages by turning their website into a multilingual booking, trust, WhatsApp follow-up, and partner-referral system.

### 6. Offer Generation

Frame the offer as a business result.

**Bad:**
> We redesign your website.

**Good:**
> We install a foreign-buyer lead system that captures serious Mexico property buyers, qualifies them, routes them to trusted agents or builders, and tracks the referral.

### 7. Demo Spec

Define:

- Screens
- Data model
- Automations
- Owner dashboard
- Customer-facing flow
- Partner/referral flow
- WhatsApp/SMS/email follow-up
- Reporting
- Monthly value loop

### 8. Outreach

Write a short message that mentions a specific opportunity and offers to show the demo.

Rules:
- Do not insult their current website.
- Do not sound generic.
- Do not pitch design first.
- Lead with money, time, trust, or operational simplicity.
- Mention one specific system idea.

### 9. Productization

If the system can be reused for 20 or more similar businesses, turn it into a niche template.

Save reusable templates under:

```text
factory/revenue-systems/{niche}/
```

### 10. Output

Produce:
- business audit
- pain scorecard
- offer
- demo spec
- outreach
- sales call script
- subscription package
- reusable niche template
- next build steps

## Self-Improvement Loop

After every completed run:

1. Append what worked to `skills/revenue-systems-agent/learnings/self-improvement-log.md`
2. Flag any new niche patterns for the library
3. Rate offer quality 1–10 based on specificity and revenue logic
4. Suggest template improvements if the same niche appeared before
