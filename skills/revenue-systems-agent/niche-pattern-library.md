## 1. Real Estate / Foreign Buyer Property Leads

### Target Customers

- Real estate agents in Mexico
- Developers and property managers
- Relocation consultants and foreign buyer advisors
- Closing coordinators and mortgage/financing brokers
- Legal/notary referral partners
- Insurance partners
- Construction/renovation partners

### End Customers

- Americans, Canadians, and foreigners buying in Mexico
- Retirees, digital nomads, investors
- Second-home buyers, families relocating, expats

### Core Pain

Foreign buyers do not know who to trust. Real estate agents lose leads because the process feels confusing, risky, and fragmented.

### System Offer: Foreign Buyer Trust & Lead Routing System

**What It Does**
- Captures foreign buyer intent and qualifies budget, timeline, location, property type
- Explains the buying process simply
- Routes leads to verified agents or partners
- Tracks referrals and supports WhatsApp follow-up
- Creates multilingual intake and trust pages for verified partners

**Screens**
1. Foreign Buyer Landing Page
2. Mexico Buyer Trust Checklist
3. Buyer Intake Form
4. Verified Agent/Partner Directory
5. Lead Routing Dashboard
6. WhatsApp Follow-up Queue
7. Partner Referral Tracker
8. Buyer Education Hub

**Automations**
- Send buyer guide
- Notify partner and create lead record
- Follow up after 1, 3, and 7 days
- Ask buyer if they connected with partner
- Request testimonial after close

### Pauli Sent Me OS Fit

**Very high.** Use: Pauli Verified partner badge, QR code for offices and signs, "Pauli sent me" lead attribution, verified agent/builder/property services pages.

### Offer

> We help Mexico real estate businesses capture and qualify serious foreign buyers, build trust before the first call, and route each lead to the right verified partner.
