# Example: Anime / Digital Product Engine

## System Name

Digital Product Factory & Fan Funnel System

## One-Line Pitch

We help creators turn anime, characters, printables, and digital assets into organized product funnels that can sell repeatedly without rebuilding from scratch.

## Funnel

```text
Fan/Buyer → Lead Magnet → Product Page → Checkout/Download → Email/Community Follow-up → Upsell → Next Drop
```

## Core Screens

1. **Product Landing Page** — Hero, product showcase, reviews, pricing, "Buy Now"
2. **Digital Asset Library** — Organized by type: prints, icons, character packs, templates
3. **Drop Calendar** — Upcoming releases, launch dates, hype building
4. **Lead Magnet Funnel** — Free resource in exchange for email
5. **Checkout/Download Page** — Payment, instant file delivery
6. **Fan CRM** — Buyers tagged by interest: anime, comics, design, etc.
7. **Creator Dashboard** — Sales, download count, email list growth
8. **Campaign Tracker** — Launch performance, revenue per drop

## Key Automations

- Publish new product
- Send launch email to list
- Tag buyers by purchase history
- Offer upsells (bundle deals, next products)
- Deliver files instantly
- Send post-purchase thank you
- Track affiliate/referral commissions

## Subscription Fit

**Growth.** This niche is fully digital and can scale beyond local businesses. Recurring revenue from product sales justifies the system monthly cost.

## Pauli Sent Me Fit

**Medium.** Less local, but can work with affiliate creators and partner communities.
