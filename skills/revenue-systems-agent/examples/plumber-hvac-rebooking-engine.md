# Example: Plumber / HVAC Lead and Maintenance Engine

## System Name

Emergency Service Lead & Maintenance Plan Engine

## One-Line Pitch

We help plumbers and HVAC companies capture urgent leads, respond faster, and turn one-time repairs into recurring maintenance customers.

## Funnel

```text
Emergency Visitor → Service Intake → Technician Alert → Status Update → Repair → Maintenance Offer → Reminder Loop → Review
```

## Core Screens

1. **Emergency Request Page** — "What's broken?" Simple form, photos, address
2. **Service Intake Form** — Contact info, urgency level, inside/outside access
3. **Photo Upload** — Customer uploads photos of the problem
4. **Technician Dispatch Dashboard** — New requests, technician assignments, ETA
5. **Customer Status Page** — Real-time: "Your technician is on the way"
6. **Maintenance Reminder Dashboard** — Seasonal: filter change, inspection, drain cleaning
7. **Review Capture Page** — Post-repair feedback and star rating

## Key Automations

- Send technician alert immediately
- Confirm customer request
- Send ETA/status update to customer
- Remind for filter change, inspection, seasonal prep
- Ask for review
- Offer maintenance plan (monthly subscription)

## Subscription Fit

**Starter, Growth, or Premium depending on volume.** One emergency call at $200+ covers the monthly fee if they close maintenance plan upsells.

## Pauli Sent Me Fit

**High.** Verified plumbers and HVAC companies are perfect for local trust directories. "Pauli sent me" becomes a referral phrase for emergency services.
