# Example: Medical / Dental / Cosmetic Tourism Engine

## System Name

Medical Tourism Trust & Patient Intake System

## One-Line Pitch

We help Mexico medical, dental, and cosmetic clinics capture qualified foreign patient leads, build trust before travel, and manage bilingual intake without losing people in the follow-up process.

## Funnel

```text
Foreign Patient → Trust Page → Treatment Intake → Coordinator Review → Quote/Consultation → Travel Checklist → Appointment → Testimonial
```

## Core Screens

1. **Treatment Landing Page** — Before/after photos, testimonials, FAQ, trust signals
2. **Patient Intake Form** — Treatment interest, health history, timeline, budget, travel readiness
3. **Trust/FAQ Page** — Answers foreign patient concerns (licensing, credentials, aftercare, financing)
4. **Procedure Eligibility Checklist** — Info to help patient qualify (health, budget, timeline)
5. **Coordinator Dashboard** — New inquiries, qualification status, quote ready/sent
6. **Quote Prep View** — Treatment plan, cost breakdown, financing options
7. **Travel Readiness Checklist** — Flight booking, hotel near clinic, post-care instructions
8. **Follow-up Tracker** — Pending info, reminder to send quote, pre-appointment confirmations

## Key Automations

- Send patient information packet on inquiry
- Notify coordinator of new lead
- Request missing documents
- Follow up after inquiry (day 3, day 7)
- Send travel checklist (1 month before)
- Send pre-appointment reminder
- Request testimonial after procedure

## Compliance Note

**This system must not provide medical advice.** It routes leads, organizes intake, and supports clinic communication. All treatment decisions belong to licensed professionals.

## Subscription Fit

**Premium or Enterprise.** One dental implant or cosmetic procedure case (typically $3k–$15k) justifies the system.

## Pauli Sent Me Fit

**High, if handled carefully.** Verification and trust are central. Pauli Verified badge for clinics builds confidence for medical tourism.
