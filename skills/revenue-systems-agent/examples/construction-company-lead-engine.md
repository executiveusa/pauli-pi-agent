# Example: Construction Company Lead Engine

## System Name

Construction Lead Qualification & Quote Intake System

## One-Line Pitch

We help construction companies stop wasting time on incomplete leads by installing a project intake system that qualifies serious customers before the first call.

## Funnel

```text
Visitor → Project Intake → Photos/Budget/Timeline → Contractor Dashboard → Quote Prep → Follow-up → Project Close → Review
```

## Core Screens

1. **Project Intake Page** — Simple form: project type, location, urgency
2. **Photo Upload** — Customer uploads photos of the space
3. **Budget/Timeline Qualifier** — "How much are you thinking?" and "When do you need this done?"
4. **Contractor Dashboard** — New projects, incomplete data, ready-to-quote
5. **Quote Prep View** — Organized photos, customer details, pre-filled quote template
6. **Follow-up Tracker** — Auto-reminders to contractors: "You have 2 quotes overdue"
7. **Review Capture** — Post-project feedback and star rating

## Key Automations

- Notify contractor immediately
- Organize lead files automatically
- Request missing info (photos, budget clarification)
- Remind contractor to follow up
- Send customer prep checklist
- Ask for review after job

## Subscription Fit

**Growth or Premium.** One serious construction project (even at 10% conversion) covers the monthly cost.
