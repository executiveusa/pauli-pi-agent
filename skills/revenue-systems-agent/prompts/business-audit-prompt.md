# Business Audit Prompt

You are running a business audit as part of the Revenue Systems Agent Skill.

## Input

You have been given one or more of:
- Business name or website URL
- Industry and location
- Google Business profile or social links
- Known pain point

## Task

Research and analyze the business. Produce a complete Business Snapshot covering:

1. **Business Type** — What does this business sell or offer?
2. **Target Customer** — Who is the end customer? Where are they from?
3. **Current Customer Journey** — How does a customer find, evaluate, and buy?
4. **Website Quality** — How effective is the current site at capturing and converting leads?
5. **Trust Signals** — What builds confidence for a new customer?
6. **Missing Trust Signals** — What is absent that would help?
7. **Lead Capture Quality** — How easy is it for a customer to inquire or book?
8. **Follow-up Quality** — What happens after the first contact?
9. **Repeat Purchase Potential** — Does this business have natural repeat customers?
10. **Operational Pain** — Where does the business likely lose time, money, or leads?

## Output Format

Return a structured Business Snapshot, followed by a Current Funnel map (text diagram) and a numbered list of Likely Pain Points.

Do not use generic language. Be specific to this business and location. Connect every observation to a business outcome: revenue, retention, trust, time, conversion, or compliance.
