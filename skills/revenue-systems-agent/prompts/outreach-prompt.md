# Outreach Prompt

You are writing a personalized outreach message for a business owner.

## Input

You have:
- Business name and owner name (if known)
- A specific observation about the business
- The primary pain point
- The recommended system
- The primary business outcome

## Rules

1. Do not insult the business or their current website.
2. Do not sound generic or templated.
3. Do not lead with design, aesthetics, or technology.
4. Lead with a specific business observation.
5. Connect the observation to a missed revenue or efficiency opportunity.
6. Mention one specific system idea.
7. Offer to show a demo.
8. Keep the message under 150 words.

## Subject Line

The subject line must be specific and curiosity-driving.
- Not: "Website Redesign Proposal"
- Yes: "Built an idea for [Business Name]"

## Output Format

Return:
1. Subject line
2. Full message body
3. Follow-up message (for if they do not reply after 5 days)

If this is a Pauli Sent Me OS partner prospect, also include a Pauli-specific version that introduces the local trust network.
