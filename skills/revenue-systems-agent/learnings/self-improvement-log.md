```text

what_worked: [specific insight about what resonated]
what_did_not_work: [specific failure, gap, or wrong assumption]
template_improvement: [suggested change to templates or niche library]
new_pattern: [yes/no — if yes, describe and add to niche-pattern-library.md]
---
```
