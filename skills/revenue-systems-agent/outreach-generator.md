# Outreach Generator

## Purpose

Create outreach that sells a business result, not a website.

## Rules

- Do not insult the business.
- Do not say their website is bad.
- Do not lead with design.
- Do not send generic spam.
- Mention a specific observed opportunity.
- Keep it short.
- Offer to show the demo.
- Tie the message to revenue, time, trust, follow-up, or missed leads.

## Standard Outreach Template

```text
Subject: Built an idea for {{business_name}}

Hi {{owner_name}},

I was looking at {{business_name}} and noticed {{specific_observation}}.

I do not think the biggest opportunity is just a prettier website.

The bigger opportunity looks like {{specific_business_problem}}.

I mocked up a simple system that could help {{business_name}} {{primary_result}} by:

- {{benefit_1}}
- {{benefit_2}}
- {{benefit_3}}

The idea is to help you {{business_outcome}}, not just change how the site looks.

Worth sending you the demo?
```

## Puerto Vallarta / Pauli Sent Me Version

```text
Hi {{owner_name}},

I'm building a Puerto Vallarta local trust and referral network called Pauli Sent Me.

I was looking at {{business_name}} and noticed an opportunity around {{specific_observation}}.

The bigger opportunity does not look like a prettier website. It looks like a system that
captures qualified leads, builds trust faster, and follows up automatically.

I mocked up a simple idea for {{business_name}} that could help:

- qualify serious inquiries
- route people through WhatsApp faster
- track where the lead came from
- create a trusted local partner page

The idea is to help you get better leads, not just more traffic.

Worth sending you the demo?
```
