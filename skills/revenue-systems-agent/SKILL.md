# Revenue Systems Agent Skill

## Mission

Use this skill to analyze a small business, directory partner, nonprofit, local service provider, tourism operator, real estate lead opportunity, or digital product niche and create a sellable software system offer.

The goal is not to sell a prettier website.

The goal is to identify a business pain point and package a simple custom system that increases revenue, improves retention, saves time, simplifies operations, improves trust, or improves compliance.

## Core Belief

Small business owners do not primarily want websites.

They want:

- more leads
- more sales
- more bookings
- better organization
- repeat customers
- less admin work
- less software confusion
- fewer missed follow-ups
- better client experiences
- a system that makes their business easier to run

## Required Workflow

1. Research the business.
2. Map the current customer journey.
3. Identify operational pain.
4. Score pain points.
5. Choose the best software-system opportunity.
6. Generate a niche-specific offer.
7. Generate a demo spec.
8. Generate outreach.
9. Generate sales-call talking points.
10. Decide whether this can become a reusable niche template.

## Quality Rules

- Be specific.
- Do not use generic marketing language.
- Tie every recommendation to money, time, trust, retention, conversion, compliance, or operational simplicity.
- Prefer systems that can be reused across 20 or more similar businesses.
- Prefer subscription value over one-time website builds.
- Prefer simple owner dashboards over bloated generic SaaS.
- Prefer trust-building systems in Puerto Vallarta and Mexico-facing foreign buyer/tourism markets.
- Prefer Pauli Sent Me OS referral loops where relevant.
