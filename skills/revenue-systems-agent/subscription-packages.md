# Subscription Packages

## Purpose

Turn one-time website work into recurring revenue.

## Rule

The monthly subscription must pay for ongoing business value, not just hosting.

Monthly value can include:

- lead tracking
- CRM
- follow-up automations
- WhatsApp/email/SMS workflows
- reporting
- campaign updates
- referral tracking
- partner pages
- maintenance
- conversion improvements
- seasonal offers
- directory placement
- Pauli Verified trust badge
- QR code tracking
- monthly business review

## Starter — $250/month

Best for small local businesses.

**Includes:**
- hosted landing/system page
- basic lead capture
- simple CRM
- basic follow-up automation
- monthly maintenance
- basic reporting

## Growth — $500/month

Best for businesses with regular leads.

**Includes:**
- everything in Starter
- owner dashboard
- WhatsApp/email follow-up queue
- lead qualification
- review capture
- monthly campaign update
- Pauli Sent Me referral tracking

## Premium — $1,000+/month

Best for high-ticket niches.

**Includes:**
- everything in Growth
- partner routing
- package builder or quote builder
- advanced CRM
- multilingual intake
- custom automations
- monthly optimization report
- directory placement
- premium trust page

## Enterprise — Custom

Best for hotels, resorts, real estate groups, clinics, and multi-partner networks.

**Includes:**
- multi-location or multi-partner dashboard
- partner referral tracking
- custom integrations
- advanced reporting
- dedicated workflow design
- lead routing rules
- API access where needed

## Pricing Rule

Use expected ROI.

If one extra sale covers the subscription, the offer is strong.

**Examples:**

- one wedding/event lead can justify Premium
- one dental/medical tourism case can justify Premium
- one real estate buyer referral can justify Premium
- one construction project can justify Premium
- recurring HVAC maintenance can justify Growth
- digital products can justify Growth if the funnel compounds
