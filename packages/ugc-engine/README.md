# UGC Character & Content Engine

This package manages the generation of fictional brand-avatars, content scripts, director shot lists, and voice configurations.

## Architecture
* **Profiles**: Fictional, approved characters (e.g. `Sofia` travel concierge).
* **Script Generation**: Creates hooks and bodies for promotion.
* **Shot List Builder**: Maps Visual cues to corresponding Audio lines.
* **Postiz adapter integration**: Exports campaign drafts directly to Postiz schedules.

## Compliance
Do not generate deceptive testimonials. UGC personas are clearly marked as fictional brand-avatars. Client-facing postings require human approval.
