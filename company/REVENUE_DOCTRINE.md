# REVENUE_DOCTRINE.md - Passive Monetization & Offers

## Monetization Rules

### 1. High Margins First
Prioritize Referral/Affiliate channels (Booking.com, Amazon affiliate storefronts) which require zero inventory and zero post-sale logistics.

### 2. Standard productization
Package complex custom coding tasks into modular Care Retainer monthly subscriptions. Sell the *agent service* rather than single one-off websites.

### 3. Pricing Retainers
* **Synthia Smart Site**: Setup fee + monthly hosting/care retainer.
* **AI Tool-Enhanced Site**: Higher setup fee + monthly automation service fee.
* **AI Receptionist widget**: Monthly subscription for routing inbound text and SMS missed calls.
* **UGC character calendar**: Monthly content scheduling and analytics retainers.
