# MISSION.md - Strategic Goals & Timeline Targets

Generate recurring monthly revenue (MRR) from autonomous digital products while building open-source, durable, and highly cost-efficient agent infrastructure.

* Onboard 25 local B2B care plan retainer subscriptions, completely fulfilled and monitored autonomously by our 16-agent team.

### 4. 5-Year Goal
* Power a decentralized swarm of self-improving AI-driven local directories and microservices that operate 24/7 across multiple languages and VPS instances.
